As fate would have it

Childhood friends Link and Zelda confront their destinies in this earliest saga of the Legend of Zelda™ series.

The Ancient Battle

This is a tale passed down through uncounted generations. Thousands of years later, some believe this legendary conflict quietly rages on…

One fateful day, the earth cracked wide and evil forces rushed forth. Their brutal assault drove the land into despair.

They did this in their lust to take the ultimate power protected by Her Grace, the Goddess—a power without equal.

A screenshot of Skyloft, home to the humans.

The Goddess gathered the surviving humans on an outcropping of earth and sent it skyward, beyond the reach of the demonic hordes.

The Goddess joined forces with the remaining tribes on the surface to seal away the evil, restoring peace.

After thousands of years, the surface world was forgotten by the humans above...

Screenshot of Link ready to plunge his sword into a stone pedestal.

Now, a new legend bound to this great story begins—forged by your own hand, with Link and Zelda at heart.

Figures of legend

Meet those who propel the world towards incredible destiny.

  • Link

    Our young hero and wielder of the Goddess Sword. Link bravely ventures down to the long-lost surface world to find his dear friend Zelda after she is knocked out of the sky.

  • Zelda

    Best friends with Link since childhood. Zelda usually watches out for Link, but she’s gone missing after being caught in a sudden, mysterious windstorm.

  • The Goddess

    Suffering great injuries in the ancient battle, the Goddess abandoned her divinity to become mortal and seal away the evil. The people of Skyloft continue to worship her legend.

  • The Imprisoned

    A scaly, vicious beast trapped in the Sealed Grounds. It is of paramount importance this beast remains chained.

  • Fi

    This calculating spirit of the Goddess Sword was created to assist the Goddess’ chosen hero through the ages.

  • Ghirahim

    A flamboyant and erratic self-styled "Demon Lord" working tirelessly on the surface world to fulfill his nefarious plans.
